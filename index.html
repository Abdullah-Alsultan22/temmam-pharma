Who We Are

Temmam Pharma is a Full-Solution Distributor for Medical and Life Science Technologies in Yemen. With offices in Sana'a, Taiz, Aden and Hadramout. We import, market, and distribute products to all over the Yemeni pharmaceutical market. We work with many companies through various agreements, partnerships and joint ventures, the group aims to be a leading healthcare, medical equipment and consumer care company in Yemen, and is committed to meeting the growing needs of healthcare and pharmaceutical distribution in the country. Temmam Pharma has a unique distribution and marketing network of more than 50 sales and medical representatives team that has been hand-picked and is highly qualified to lead a path of success that ensures submitting results of high standards to our partners.
